About Us

Balay Gulay, whose name comes from the Ilocano word "Balay" meaning house. This small but dedicated supplier focuses on promoting healthy living by offering fresh and accessible varieties of vegetables to the community.

The family behind Balay Gulay remains committed to their mission of making nutritious food options available to everyone. As they continue to grow and prosper, they maintain their core values of freshness, accessibility, and community service.

History

Balay Gulay started in 2015 as a small scale walk in vegetable store exclusive to the residents of Village East. Its humble beginnings focused on providing fresh, locally sourced produce to nearby houses. As months went by, Balay Gulay gradually expanded its reach by offering delivery services outside the village, allowing more customers to enjoy its fresh vegetables.

However, during the pandemic, the walk-in store was forced to close due to health and safety restrictions. Despite the challenges, Balay Gulay continued its operations through delivery services, ensuring that customers still had access to fresh and healthy vegetables during those difficult times.

In 2022, Balay Gulay made a comeback by reopening its walk-in store, marking a new chapter of growth and community connection. Unfortunately, due to unforeseen issues and challenges, the store had to permanently close its physical location.
